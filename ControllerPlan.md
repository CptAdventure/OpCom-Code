Code for the controls:

- M = Motor
- S = Servo

- Hook: S
- Lower Hook: LB
- Raise Hook: LB

- Vertical Claw Movement: M
- Lower Claw: D-Pad Up
- Raise Claw: D-Pad Down

- Claw Grab: S
- Open Claw: RB
- Close Claw: RB

- Claw Extension: M
- Extend Claw: D-Pad Left
- Retract Claw: D-Pad Right

- Intake: M
- Start Intake: A
- Stop Intake: A
- Unjam Intake: B
